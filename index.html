A collection of 9,999 sakebaros

A sakebaro is algorithmically generated NFTs.
Each sakebaro is uniquely generated from 8 types of face / eye / mouth components.
You will meet your sakebaro when you mint.
To participate in minting, please link MetaMask from Connect Wallet.

All elements stored on the Ethereum blockchain

We have developed a way to store sakebaro images on the ETH network directly.
Sakebaros will be stored as long as the ETH network exists.
We believe that it would be wonderful if sakebaro could remain intact in the future and be passed on to our children.
