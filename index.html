Mission

Our mission is to spread sake as a culture to the world.

We have noticed that there is still a gap between what people around the world know as Japanese sake and what we know as sake in Japan.
We believe that what is important when enjoying sake is not only the sake itself, but also creating a space to drink it.

We currently have a sake bar in Nakameguro, Tokyo. Future plans include a new location in Omotesando in December, and in Kyoto spring 2022.
After that, our ambition is to spread sake culture to locations such as Paris, Hong Kong, and other cities around the world.
Please check sakebaro's official Instagram for more information.
